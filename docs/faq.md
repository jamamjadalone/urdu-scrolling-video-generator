## General Questions

**Q: Is this tool really free?**
A: Yes, 100% free, forever. No hidden costs, ads, or premium versions.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No account needed. Works directly in your browser without signup.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Nowhere! All content stays on your device. We don't collect or store any data.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: Yes, it works on mobile browsers, but desktop is recommended for recording.

## Monetization Questions

**Q: Will YouTube flag my videos as duplicate?**
A: No! Because each video has unique colors, fonts, and animations - YouTube sees original content.

**Q: How long until I can monetize?**
A: YouTube requires 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours. This tool helps you create content faster.

**Q: Can I use this commercially?**
A: Yes, for YouTube channels, TikTok, Instagram, or any commercial use.

**Q: What about copyright issues?**
A: You're responsible for your content. Use only original or properly licensed Urdu text.

## Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and most modern browsers.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: It works without internet once loaded, but needs internet initially to access.

**Q: What's the maximum preview size?**
A: Theoretically unlimited - drag to resize as needed.

**Q: Can I use external fonts?**
A: Currently supports 4 Urdu fonts. Additional fonts can be added via browser.

## Recording Questions

**Q: What screen recorder should I use?**
A: OBS Studio (free), Camtasia (professional), or native recorders (Windows/Mac).

**Q: What video resolution should I use?**
A: 1920x1080 (1080p) for YouTube best quality.

**Q: Can I record directly from the tool?**
A: No, use a screen recorder. The tool provides the animation, recorder captures it.

**Q: How long should videos be?**
A: 2-5 minutes ideal. Longer videos need more content/editing.

## Customization Questions

**Q: Can I change fonts after recording?**
A: Yes, but you'd need to re-record. Better to decide beforehand.

**Q: Are there more than 4 fonts?**
A: Four are built-in. CSS allows custom fonts if you want to edit code.

**Q: Can I animate multiple texts?**
A: Currently scrolls Q/A or Story one at a time. Good for focused content.

**Q: Can I add images or videos?**
A: Only as background. Main content is text-based for scrolling effect.

## Performance Questions

**Q: Why is animation stuttering?**
A: Close other apps, clear cache, or reduce animation speed.

**Q: Will it slow down my computer?**
A: No, it's a web tool. Uses minimal resources.

**Q: Can I batch process multiple videos?**
A: Not automatically, but you can create many videos one after another.

**Q: What if I want to make changes?**
A: Simply change settings and re-record. No permanent changes.

## Output Questions

**Q: What video formats can I export?**
A: Whatever your screen recorder supports (MP4, MOV, AVI, etc.). MP4 recommended.

**Q: Can I edit the output video?**
A: Yes! Edit in Adobe Premiere, DaVinci Resolve, CapCut, etc.

**Q: How do I add music?**
A: Record separately or add during video editing.

**Q: Can I add subtitles?**
A: Add during editing in your video editor.

## Monetization Strategy Questions

**Q: How often should I post?**
A: Daily for faster growth (if you have time to create content).

**Q: What content performs best?**
A: Educational, entertaining Q&A, funny jokes, and motivational content.

**Q: How long to get 1000 subs?**
A: Depends on content quality and promotion. Typically 1-6 months.

**Q: What's a reasonable monetization timeline?**
A: 2-3 months with consistent daily uploads of quality content.

## Troubleshooting Questions

**Q: Text not showing in preview?**
A: Check color (too light), increase size, or refresh browser.

**Q: Font looks weird?**
A: Try different font, check browser supports Urdu, clear cache.

**Q: Preview won't resize?**
A: Drag from bottom-right corner, use modern browser.

**Q: Play button won't work?**
A: First click "Setup Preview", then PLAY.

**Q: Recording captures but no sound?**
A: Enable audio in screen recorder settings or add during editing.
