## YouTube Algorithm Tips

### Video Length
- Under 3 min: 60-70% completion rate
- 3-5 min: 50-60% completion rate
- 5-10 min: 40-50% completion rate
- 10+ min: 30-40% completion rate

**Recommendation:** Start with 2-5 minute videos for better algorithm favor.

### Upload Schedule
- Morning (9 AM): Good engagement
- Afternoon (3 PM): Peak traffic
- Evening (6-8 PM): Maximum views
- Consistency matters more than timing

### Keywords & SEO
- Use primary keyword in title (first 3 words)
- Include keywords in description
- Use relevant tags (10-12 tags)
- Add community posts
- Pin important comments

## Growing Subscribers Fast

### The 100 Subscriber Milestone
- Get friends/family to subscribe
- Cross-promote on social media
- Create quality content
- Engage with community
- Typical timeline: 2-4 weeks

### 1000 Subscriber Milestone
- Consistent 3-5 uploads/week
- 70%+ average view duration
- Active community engagement
- Quality thumbnails & titles
- Typical timeline: 3-6 months

### 10,000 Subscriber Milestone
- 5-7 uploads per week
- 3-5 minute optimal length
- Trending topic coverage
- Collaborations with other creators
- Typical timeline: 6-12 months

### 100,000 Subscriber Milestone
- Daily uploads (burnout risk)
- 50-70% viewer retention
- Merchandise or Patreon
- Professional thumbnails
- Typical timeline: 1-2 years

## Monetization Optimization

### CPM Improvement Strategies

**High CPM Content** ($3-8 per 1000 views)
- English content with international audience
- Tech and finance videos
- US/UK targeted content

**Medium CPM Content** ($1-3 per 1000 views)
- General Urdu content
- Educational videos
- Entertainment

**Low CPM Content** ($0.25-1 per 1000 views)
- Short clips
- Low production quality
- Mobile views only

**Recommendation:** Mix content for optimal CPM.

### RPM Optimization
- RPM = Your actual earnings per 1000 views
- Goal: Maximize watch time (ads shown to engaged viewers)
- Strategy: Create content that keeps people watching
